Serial founder with three exits and 20+ years building ventures and innovation ecosystems across Asia. Neelesh founded AKADASIA, Southeast Asia's largest educator community. thegurucool is his second venture built for teachers, in a market he knows first-hand.

  • Founded AKADASIA, Southeast Asia's largest educator community

More about Neelesh

Neelesh has spent two decades at the intersection of education and entrepreneurship in Asia: teaching at Singapore Polytechnic, designing national-scale incubation programmes, and building products that put educators first. A regular speaker at EDUtech Asia and across the regional edtech circuit, he brings an unmatched network of schools, universities and education ministries to thegurucool's mission of preparing every teacher for the AI era.
