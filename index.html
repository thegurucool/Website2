The TEACH-AI Framework

Grounded in pedagogy.
Built for SEA classrooms.

TEACH-AI™ doesn't just reference educational frameworks, it turns them into measurable competencies, personalised learning pathways, and verified outcomes. The TEACH-AI™ framework brings together the world's most trusted educational research and transforms it into a system that actively develops, measures, and validates teacher growth.
This is not a theory. This is applied, trackable progress.

Built on the world's most trusted frameworks
Bloom's Taxonomy
From recall to creation.
We don't just assess knowledge, we help teachers progress through higher-order thinking and instructional design.
Anderson and Krathwohl, 2001
TPACK
Technology meets pedagogy and content.
We evaluate how teachers integrate AI and digital tools meaningfully, not just use them.
Mishra and Koehler, 2006
Universal Design for Learning
Designed for every learner.
Our competencies embed inclusive and adaptive teaching practices across all domains.
CAST, 2018
Hattie's Visible Learning
Focused on what actually works.
We prioritise high-impact teaching practices backed by evidence, not trends.
Hattie, 2009

How it works

From insight to verified mastery in four steps

1

See where you really stand

A fast, scenario-based diagnostic across 7 domains gives you a clear, unbiased view of your teaching strengths and gaps, benchmarked against real-world expectations.

2

Get a path that's actually yours

Your personalised learning pathway adapts to your role, your gaps, and your classroom context. No generic modules. No wasted time.

3

Build skills that show up in your classroom

Bite-sized, practical learning designed for real teaching environments. GuruCool, your personal AI Tutor, supports you anytime, helping you apply what you learn immediately.

4

Turn progress into proof

Earn verified credentials as you master each domain. Build a professional portfolio that shows what you can do, not just what you've attended.
