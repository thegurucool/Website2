The TEACH-AI Framework

Grounded in pedagogy.
Built for SEA classrooms.

The TEACH-AI framework is not another AI checklist. Every domain maps directly to established educational research, the same frameworks trusted by schools and ministries worldwide, adapted for the realities of K-12 teaching in Southeast Asia and India.

Built on the shoulders of
Bloom's Taxonomy
Cognitive levels from recall to creation. Our assessments and learning paths are designed to move teachers up the taxonomy, not just test recall.
Anderson and Krathwohl, 2001
TPACK
Technological Pedagogical Content Knowledge. The gold standard for understanding how teachers integrate technology meaningfully into subject teaching.
Mishra and Koehler, 2006
Universal Design for Learning
A framework for designing flexible learning experiences that reach every student. Embedded across our Inclusion and Adaptive Teaching domains.
CAST, 2018
Hattie's Visible Learning
The largest synthesis of education research in history. Our competency design prioritises the high-effect-size practices that evidence shows actually move student outcomes.
Hattie, 2009
